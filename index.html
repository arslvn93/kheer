At Kheer.ca, we are passionate about sharing the rich culinary heritage of Pakistan. Our mission is to bring the comfort, joy, and authentic taste of homemade Kheer right to your doorstep, freshly prepared for each order.

our story

Our story began when we moved to Canada from Pakistan in 1999, carrying our family's treasured Kheer recipe. Since then, we've been making this delightful dessert in our Mississauga home, sharing the taste of Pakistani tradition with our local community.

FAQS

What is Kheer?

Kheer is a traditional Pakistani dessert, a type of rice pudding lovingly created with cardamom, nuts, and sometimes a dash of rose water for a unique aromatic experience.

How is your Kheer prepared?

Our Kheer is made following our family's cherished recipe. It's prepared with premium ingredients and cooked with utmost care to ensure the best flavour and quality.

When should I consume the Kheer?

Our Kheer is freshly prepared upon each order and should ideally be consumed within 48 hours of delivery to experience its optimal taste and quality.
